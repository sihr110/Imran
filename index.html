📖 About IRC

The Islamic Research Center (IRC) was established in Karachi to promote authentic Islamic knowledge based on the Quran and Sunnah.

Our mission is to serve the Muslim community of Karachi and surrounding areas by providing religious education, social services, and a place for worship.

Location:  F B Area Block 6, Karachi, Pakistan
