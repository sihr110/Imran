Welcome to IRC Islamic Center

The Islamic Research Center (IRC) in Karachi is dedicated to providing authentic Islamic education, fostering community spirit, and serving humanity. We welcome everyone - Muslims and non-Muslims - to learn about Islam in a peaceful, respectful environment.

📖 About IRC

The Islamic Research Center (IRC) was established in Karachi to promote authentic Islamic knowledge based on the Quran and Sunnah.

Our mission is to serve the Muslim community of Karachi and surrounding areas by providing religious education, social services, and a place for worship.

Location: Block 7, Gulshan-e-Iqbal, Karachi, Pakistan
