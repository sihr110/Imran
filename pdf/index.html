📖 About IRC

The Islamic Research Center (IRC) was established in Berlin, Connecticut to promote authentic Islamic knowledge based on the Quran and Sunnah.

Our mission is to serve the Muslim community of Berlin and surrounding areas in Connecticut by providing religious education, social services, and a place for worship.
